Jaya Prakash Champati leads the Edge AI Research Group at the University of Victoria. His research focuses on decision-making, learning, and resource allocation problems in networking and information systems, with current work spanning hierarchical inference, inference offloading, and Age of Information.

Before joining UVic, he was a Research Assistant Professor at IMDEA Networks Institute from 2021 to 2024 and a postdoctoral researcher at KTH Royal Institute of Technology. Earlier in his career, he worked in industry on LTE MAC layer development at Broadcom Communications in Bangalore.
